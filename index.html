Autonomous Fuel Dispensing Robot

AFORV has been chosen as the Computer Vision partner and its roles include

  • Build an object detection model for detecting fuel lids, fuel caps and fuel inlets of motor cars that pull into a fuel station for filling up
  • Source images, train the model to detect all three objects with > 95% confidence
  • Extract X, Y and Z coordinates of detected images and use a ROS system to send the coordinates to the robotic arm

Software stack – YOLOV5, Unbuntu 18.04, CUDA 10.2, Pytorch, ROS

Environment – NVidia Jetson ZED Box, NVidia ZED2i stereo camera

Fraud Detection and Prevention of Motor Insurance Claims

Technologies has been chosen to Design, Architect, Build, Test, Deploy, Enhance and Maintain the application

  • Automate the process filling in details from Police reports, Policy inputs, Driving license, National ID from PDFs to Database
  • ML/NLP to detect and alert in cases where Claims are tagged to suspect garages, geographic regions, towing services and police officers

Software stack – Dotnet 2.0 core, Windows 11, SQL community edition, MongoDB, PaddleOCR, PaddleML, OpenCV, Python

Cloud Environment – Azure VM, Devops

Fraud Detection and Prevention of Motor Insurance using Computer Vision

Technologies has been chosen to Design, Architect, Build, Test, Deploy, Enhance and Maintain the application

  • Use FNOL Images/Videos of damaged parts in case of motor accidents
  • Compare images received from garages with images of damaged parts with FNOL images
  • Map out differences if any (visually, with coordinates) and alert Insurers representative

Software stack – Java 14, Python, MongoDB, OpenCV, Yolov7, Detectron

Cloud Environment – AWS/Azure/Google (TBD)
Radical new approach data, training and usage of CNNs & RNNs have evoked interest from Insurers from Middle East, South and East Africa, South East Asia and India in terms of participating in a Beta.
