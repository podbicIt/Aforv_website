Advisory & Audit Services

AFORV understand that the cloud can be a complex and ever-changing landscape, which is why we offer comprehensive advisory and audit services to help businesses navigate the cloud and ensure that their cloud-based systems and applications are secure, compliant, and optimized for performance.

How we do

Our Approach

Our cloud advisory and audit services, working closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, technology infrastructure, and pain points. Our team of experts combines their deep cloud knowledge and experience with industry best practices and standards to provide comprehensive advisory and audit services tailored to meet our clients' unique needs.

Our process typically includes the following steps:

Assessment

We conduct a comprehensive assessment of our clients' cloud-based systems and applications, identifying areas of risk and opportunities for improvement.

Planning

Based on the results of our assessment, we work with our clients to develop a customized cloud advisory and audit plan that is tailored to their specific needs and objectives.

Execution

Our team of experts executes the advisory and audit plan, providing guidance and recommendations on how to optimize the performance, security, and compliance of our clients' cloud-based systems and applications.

Reporting

We provide our clients with detailed reports that highlight our findings, recommendations, and actions required to optimize their cloud-based systems and applications.

What We Use

Our Technology Stack:

Technologies and tools, we use to provide our cloud advisory and audit services. Some of the technologies we work with include:

Cloud Management Platforms

We work with cloud management platforms such as CloudHealth, CloudCheckr, and Azure Cost Management to help businesses manage and optimize their cloud spend.

Cloud Security Tools

We use cloud security tools such as Azure Security Center, AWS Security Hub, and Google Cloud Security Command Center to help businesses monitor and manage their cloud security posture.

Cloud Compliance Tools

We use cloud compliance tools such as AWS Artifact, Azure Compliance Manager, and Google Cloud Compliance Manager to help businesses ensure that their cloud-based systems and applications are compliant with industry standards and regulations.

Cloud Governance Tools

We use cloud governance tools such as AWS Organizations, Azure Policy, and Google Cloud Resource Manager to help businesses establish effective policies and controls for their cloud-based systems and applications.
