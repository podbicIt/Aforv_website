Migration & Transformation

AFORV understand that migrating your business to the cloud can be a complex and challenging process. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of cloud migration and transformation services designed to help businesses migrate their applications and data to the cloud quickly, efficiently, and securely.

How we do

Our Approach

AFORV approach to cloud migration and transformation that is tailored to meet the unique needs of each of our clients. We work closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, pain points, and existing technology infrastructure, allowing us to develop migration and transformation solutions that are both effective and sustainable.

Our process typically includes the following steps:

Assessment

We conduct a comprehensive assessment of our clients' existing technology infrastructure and identify areas for improvement.

Planning

We work with our clients to develop a comprehensive cloud migration and transformation plan that is tailored to their specific needs. This may include a phased approach, prioritizing applications and data that are critical to the business.

Execution

Our team of experts works closely with our clients to execute the migration and transformation plan, ensuring that applications and data are migrated efficiently and securely.

Optimization

We work with our clients to optimize their cloud-based systems and applications to ensure that they are running smoothly and efficiently.

What We Use

Our Technology Stack:

we use a range of technologies and tools to develop and implement our cloud migration and transformation solutions. Some of the technologies we work with include:

Cloud Migration Tools

We work with cloud migration tools such as Azure Migrate, AWS Migration Hub, and Google Cloud Migrate to help businesses migrate their applications and data to the cloud quickly and efficiently.

Infrastructure as Code (IaC) Tools

We use IaC tools such as Terraform, Ansible, and CloudFormation to automate the deployment of cloud infrastructure.

DevOps Tools

We use DevOps tools such as Jenkins, Git, and Ansible to automate the deployment of applications to the cloud.

Cloud Native Services

We use cloud-native services such as AWS Lambda, Azure Functions, and Google Cloud Functions to help businesses build and deploy serverless applications in the cloud.
