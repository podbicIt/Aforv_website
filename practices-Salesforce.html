Salesforce

We specialize in helping businesses leverage the power of Salesforce to improve their sales, marketing, and customer service operations. Our team of certified Salesforce experts has years of experience developing and implementing custom solutions that can help businesses achieve their goals and drive growth.

How we do

Our Approach

AFORV take a holistic approach to Salesforce that is tailored to meet the unique needs of each of our clients. We work closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, pain points, and existing technology infrastructure, allowing us to develop solutions that are both effective and sustainable.

Our process typically includes the following steps:

Discovery

We work closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, pain points, and existing technology infrastructure. This allows us to identify opportunities for Salesforce and develop a clear roadmap for implementation.

Solution Design

We develop a comprehensive solution that is tailored to meet our clients' specific needs. This may include a combination of Salesforce products, custom development, and third-party integrations.

Implementation

Our team of experts works closely with our clients to implement the solution, ensuring that it is integrated seamlessly with their existing systems and workflows.

Training and Support

We provide training and ongoing support to ensure that our clients' employees are equipped to use Salesforce effectively.

What We Use

Our Technology Stack:

AFORV use a range of technologies and tools to develop and implement our Salesforce solutions. Some of the technologies we work with include:

Salesforce Lightning

We use Salesforce Lightning, the modern user interface for Salesforce, to develop customized user interfaces for our clients.

Apex

We use Apex, the Salesforce programming language, to develop custom applications and workflows.

Visualforce

We use Visualforce, the Salesforce web application development framework, to develop customized pages and interfaces.

Heroku

We use Heroku, a cloud-based platform, to develop and deploy custom applications on Salesforce.
