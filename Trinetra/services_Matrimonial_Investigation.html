Matrimonial Investigation

Pre-matrimonial Investigation

One of the most important decisions of your life should be a well-informed one as well. Don’t you think so?

Conducting a thorough investigation & verification about all the relevant information regarding a person’s life before marrying them is called a Pre- matrimonial investigation.

As we all know; life is result of the choices we make and the decisions we take at every step. Therefore, in order to have a happy and fulfilling life, we have to make the right choices in every aspect isn’t it?

Choosing a partner for marriage is one of the significant decisions made by a person in their life. To make the right choice in this phase, it becomes extremely important in this day and age to clarify all the relevant information about the future spouse through a reliable private detective agency. The sole purpose of this kind of investigation is to ensure that you or your loved one doesn’t get trapped by a phony person.

BENEFITS OF PRE-MATRIMONIAL INVESTIGATION: -

  • Helps you to make a well informed decision when you are choosing your life partner.
  • Helps to verify and clarify all vital facts regarding your prospective life partner’s background.
  • Acts as an alert signal, when the opposite party has a risky and suspicious lifestyle.
  • Prevents from being trapped by deceiving people.
  • Reveals true & unbiased identity of the opposite party which helps you to make a fair choice.

Post-matrimonial Investigation

People marry in haste and repent at leisure is a popular saying. It happens in quite a number of cases these days. Wedded bliss does not last forever, especially if one partner turns out to be a fraudster or develops illicit relations or gets involved in company that steers him towards gambling or other addictive activities. You need post matrimonial investigative help to find out the truth. TRINETRA INTELLIGENCE offers complete post matrimonial investigative services. If you notice a change in routine or behaviour of your spouse then you should call us for investigations and set your mind at rest.

Post matrimonial investigative services

Investigations ideally should be carried out prior to matrimony but this is not always the case, whether it is an arranged marriage fixed with a partner found through marriage portals or through love. However, spousal behaviours and attitudes can change after marriage and you may have your suspicions. Call us to set your mind at rest. We will carry out thorough investigation covering:

  • Daily routine at office and after routine office work, places visited, people contacted and so on;
  • Check if person travels and carry out surveillance of their activities wherever they go;
  • Check circle of friends and relatives of person investigated to know gossip and rumours and follow up;
  • Carry out video and audio surveillance and record meetings and take photographs discreetly;
  • Check health status to know if this or drug or alcoholism is causing change in routine and behaviours;
  • Gather incontrovertible and incontestable evidence of spousal infidelity should a client plan to initiate divorce proceedings;
  • Gather information from internet and online social activities to add to evidence.
