Pre & Post Employement Verification

TRINETRA runs hundreds of checks in the first stage of hiring itself, to save your organization’s cost, time and efforts.

Address Verification

A surprise visit to the candidate’s residential location to confirm if he/she/they actually live there.

Employment Verification

Telephonic or email screening of the candidate’s experience, position, behavior and compensation in the previous organization(s).

Educational Verification

Written confirmation from the university/college/institution on the authenticity of the mark sheet or diploma/degree shared by the candidate.

Criminal and Civil Record Check – E-Courts

Our certified team of lawyers runs hundreds of checks with the District Court, High Court or Supreme Court to confirm no civil or criminal proceedings against the candidate.

Identity Check

Confirmation by the government bodies on the authenticity of the identity documents, such as Aadhar no., Pan card or Passport of the candidate.

Professional Reference Check

Recommendation on the behavior and work ethics of the candidate from his previous employers, teachers, mentors, etc.

Global Database Check

As a registered partner with World-Check, we have direct access to their database and can scan a candidate’s information at a click.

Criminal Record Check – Police

We submit the complete set of candidate’s documents to the nearest CID office or Superintendent of Police for a certificate of authenticity of documents.

Drug Abuse Test

We collect a urine sample of the candidate and send it to an authorized laboratory for any drug consumption test.

Gap Check

Identifying any gaps in the information shared by the candidate and sourcing the valid reasons for those gaps.

Credit Record Check

We check the credit bureau to cross-check any credit frauds made by the candidate in the past.

Social Media Checks

Detailed observation of the candidate’s Facebook profile or a Google search allows insights into the candidate’s mindset that may not be possible with just a resume.

Resume Check

A candidate may sometimes put false education history, compensation or employment history to mislead HR and get the job.
